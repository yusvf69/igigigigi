## 2. Mission

### 2.1 Mission Statement

Transform how the world learns about Islam, history, science, and culture through AI-powered personalized education. Empower learners to deepen their understanding, strengthen their faith, and grow their knowledge throughout their lives.

### 2.2 Mission Objectives

| Objective | Key Result | Timeline |
|-----------|------------|----------|
| **Accessibility** | Platform available in 20+ languages covering 85% of the global Muslim population | Year 5 |
| **Engagement** | Average learner spends 30+ minutes per session, 4+ sessions per week | Year 3 |
| **Retention** | 90-day retention rate > 60% for active users | Year 3 |
| **Accuracy** | 100% of core Islamic content scholar-verified before publication | Ongoing |
| **Personalization** | AI-driven adaptive learning paths for every learner | Year 2 |
| **Completeness** | Cover all major Islamic disciplines with 10,000+ hours of learning content | Year 5 |

### 2.3 Target Audiences

| Audience | Size (Global) | Primary Need | Primary Offering |
|----------|---------------|--------------|------------------|
| Muslim Youth (8-18) | 600M | Engaging Islamic education | Gamified learning, stories, challenges |
| Young Adults (18-30) | 500M | Deepening understanding | Structured courses, AI mentor, community |
| Adults (30-60) | 600M | Lifelong learning, family | Family plans, parent dashboard, podcasts |
| New Muslims / Converts | 50M | Foundational learning | Guided pathways, mentor support, community |
| Scholars & Students | 20M | Advanced study | Research tools, citation export, library access |

| Institutions (Schools) | 100K+ | Curriculum delivery | School subscription, teacher dashboard |
| Institutions (Universities) | 5K+ | Course integration | LMS integration, research access |

### 2.4 Educational Philosophy

| Principle | Application |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Learner-Centric** | Every feature serves learner outcomes, not engagement metrics alone |
| **Multi-Perspective** | Present multiple scholarly views with proper attribution, never a single interpretation |
| **Progressive Disclosure** | Start simple, deepen complexity as learner grows |
| **Active Learning** | Minimize passive consumption; maximize interaction, reflection, and practice |
| **Spiritual Connection** | Education should strengthen faith, not just transfer information |
| **Lifelong Journey** | Design for learning across all life stages, from childhood to old age |

## 4. Monetization

### 4.1 Monetization Philosophy

| Principle | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Knowledge as Mercy** | Islamic knowledge should never be withheld due to inability to pay |
| **Value-Based Pricing** | Premium features priced on value delivered, not cost-plus |
| **No Riba** | No interest-based financing or late fees |
| **No Gharar** | Clear, transparent, fixed pricing — no hidden fees |
| **Zakat-Compatible** | Platform subscriptions eligible for zakat distribution |
| **Fair Compensation** | Scholars, creators, and contributors equitably compensated |

## 6. Marketplace

### 6.1 Marketplace Overview

The Content Creator Marketplace enables third-party scholars, teachers, and content creators to publish educational content on the platform, expanding the content library while creating economic opportunities for the global Islamic education community.

| Element | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Purpose** | Enable third-party content creation and distribution at scale |
| **Launch Date** | Year 2, Q4 (pilot with 50 invited creators) |
| **Full Launch** | Year 3, Q1 (open to all verified creators) |
| **Content Types** | Courses, lesson plans, flashcard decks, quiz banks, podcast episodes, documentary scripts, study guides |
| **Quality Model** | Multi-layer review before publication |
| **Revenue Model** | Revenue split: 70% creator, 30% platform |

### 6.2 Creator Onboarding

| Step | Description | Verification Required |
|------|-------------|---------------------|
| 1. Account Registration | Basic profile, portfolio, tax information | Identity verification |
| 2. Credential Verification | Academic credentials, scholarly certifications | Degree/certificate verification |
| 3. Platform Training | Content creation guidelines, platform tools training | Training completion |
| 4. Sample Submission | Submit sample content for quality review | Quality team assessment |
| 5. Contract Execution | Creator agreement signed (digital signature) | Legal verification |
| 6. Profile Activation | Creator profile goes live | Profile completeness check |
| 7. First Publication | Submit first piece of content | Standard quality review |

### 6.3 Creator Tiers

| Tier | Criteria | Commission | Benefits |
|------|----------|------------|----------|
| **Emerging** | < 10 published items, < 1,000 total enrollments | 65% creator / 35% platform | Basic analytics, standard support |
| **Established** | 10+ published items, 1K-10K enrollments, 4.0+ rating | 70% creator / 30% platform | Advanced analytics, priority review |
| **Featured** | 50+ published items, 10K+ enrollments, 4.5+ rating | 75% creator / 25% platform | Marketing support, featured placement, dedicated account manager |
| **Scholar** | Verified Islamic scholar, scholarly credentials | 80% creator / 20% platform | Premium placement, platform promotion, exclusive badges |
| **Institutional** | University, research center, accredited institution | 80% creator / 20% platform | Bulk content publishing, API access, custom branding |

### 6.4 Quality Review Process

| Stage | Description | Reviewer | Timeline |
|-------|-------------|----------|----------|
| Automated Scan | Format check, completeness, plagiarism scan, AI detection | AI System | < 5 minutes |
| Content Review | Accuracy, quality, engagement, pedagogical soundness | Content Review Team | < 48 hours |
| Religious Verification | Islamic accuracy, source attribution, scholarly consensus | Religious Review Team | < 72 hours (routine) / < 1 week (complex) |
| Editorial Review | Language quality, clarity, formatting, accessibility | Editorial Team | < 24 hours |
| Final Approval | All checks passed, publication scheduling | Quality System | Automated |

**Quality Review Standards:**
- Every fact must cite a verifiable source
- No contradictory or fabricated hadith
- Scholarly opinions must be attributed to specific scholars/schools
- Content must distinguish between core beliefs and scholarly opinions
- Language must be respectful and appropriate for all audiences
- No sectarian bias or polemical content
- Appropriate for designated age/education level

### 6.5 Creator Rating & Review System

| Element | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Creator Rating** | 1-5 stars based on content quality, accuracy, engagement |
| **Learner Reviews** | Verified purchasers can leave text reviews |
| **Review Moderation** | All reviews moderated for appropriateness within 24 hours |
| **Rating Factors** | Content accuracy (40%), engagement (25%), clarity (20%), value (15%) |
| **Creator Response** | Creators can respond to reviews publicly |
