## 1. EXECUTIVE OVERVIEW

### 1.1 Purpose

The Future Roadmap charts the strategic evolution of the Islamic Education Platform from its initial launch through four phases of expansion, outlining major features, technical improvements, content growth targets, and long-term vision.

### 1.2 Core Principle

**Build the foundation right, then expand thoughtfully. Quality and authenticity always precede speed and scale.**

---

## 2. ROADMAP PHILOSOPHY

| Principle | Description |
|---|---|
| **Value-Driven** | Every feature must directly improve learner outcomes |
| **Feedback-Informed** | Roadmap shaped by learner data and community input |
| **Sustainable Pace** | Realistic timelines that maintain quality |
| **Islamic Alignment** | Growth consistent with Islamic values and scholarly guidance |
| **Technical Excellence** | Pay down technical debt continuously |

## 8. RISKS AND MITIGATION

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|---|
| **AI Hallucination** | Medium | Critical | Multi-layer verification, Knowledge Graph grounding, human review |
| **Content Accuracy Disputes** | Medium | High | Scholarly consensus approach, multiple views presented |
| **Scaling Challenges** | Medium | High | Cloud-native architecture, auto-scaling, performance budgets |
| **Funding/Sustainability** | Low | High | Subscription model, institutional licensing, waqf-funded scholarships |
| **Regulatory Changes** | Low | Medium | Legal monitoring, flexible compliance framework |
| **Talent Retention** | Medium | Medium | Competitive compensation, mission-driven culture, remote-friendly |
